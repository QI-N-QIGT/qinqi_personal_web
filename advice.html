Life Advice

Fast Iteration for Long-term Goals

Pursue long-term goals, but don't fall into the trap of perfectionism. Embrace rapid iteration and continuous improvement. Speed and agility often triumph over meticulous craftsmanship when building towards ambitious objectives.

Aim High, Dream Big

Set your sights on the highest peaks. As the ancient Chinese wisdom goes: "Aim for the top and you'll get the middle; aim for the middle and you'll get the bottom." Ambitious goals, even if not fully achieved, lead to extraordinary outcomes.
